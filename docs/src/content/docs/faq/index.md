A starter FAQ. Everything in this section is general-audience; the
operator-flavoured "I tried X and Y broke" questions live in
[Common inquiries](/faq/inquiries/).

## I'm having difficulty installing SourceBans++, can you help me?

We'd love to! But first, please read through the
[Quickstart](/getting-started/quickstart/),
[Common inquiries](/faq/inquiries/), and the
[Troubleshooting](/troubleshooting/database-errors/) section.

If you're still stuck after that, ask in the relevant help channel of
our [Discord](https://discord.gg/4Bhj6NU).

## Can I use SourceBans++ without the WebPanel?

At minimum you need the WebPanel to **install / configure** SB++ and
to add your game servers to the panel. In theory past that point you
could stop using the panel and SB++ would continue to work — but
you'd lose access to lots of core functionality (adding new servers,
creating new admins, modifying bans, handling appeals).

For that reason we strongly recommend running the entire package,
WebPanel included, at all times.

## I want to contribute!

That's great! Take a stab at the triaged
[issues](https://github.com/sbpp/sourcebans-pp/issues) labelled
`good first issue` or `help wanted`. The
[`AGENTS.md`](https://github.com/sbpp/sourcebans-pp/blob/main/AGENTS.md)
in the repo root is the contributor cheatsheet — conventions, dev
commands, and the "where to find what" index.
